The Facility

It's three main areas

There's two indoor facilities. Actual Fitness 1 is smaller, and is designed to be a private training studio. It's got all the equipment you need. Actual Fitness 2 is larger, and designed for classes with lots of folks in them.

And then, there's the Prison Yard. The Prison Yard is an outdoor training facility with all kinds of goodies, like tires for flippin' and hammers for hittin'. Lots of bodyweight training exercises can be done here.
